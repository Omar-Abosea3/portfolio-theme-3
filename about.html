About Our Company

Building trust through people, technology and service excellence since 2008.

Our Story

ManPro Solutions began as a small recruitment desk and grew into a full-service partner offering manpower, ecommerce and booking solutions to enterprises across the region.

Today we serve over 1,500 organizations, placing more than 20,000 workers and powering hundreds of online storefronts and booking experiences.
